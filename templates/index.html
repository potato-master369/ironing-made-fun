Welcome to Ironing Made Fun! This initiative was created to bring a light-hearted twist to one of life’s more mundane tasks—ironing. Through a playful reward system, we aim to make the experience more enjoyable, productive, and maybe even a little entertaining.

This proof of concept is a collaborative project by Yuhann, Zhang Chi, and Yuhao, originally prototyped on Scratch (users: hannyu13, helloed123, and oahuygnaw). Since then, it has evolved into a more refined demonstration of how gamification can be applied to everyday routines.

We hope you enjoy exploring the project as much as we enjoyed building it. Thank you for visiting, and may your creases always be intentional.
